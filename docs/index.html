Privacy Policy

Refugee Literacy Project · Last updated: February 27, 2026

Summary: This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It works entirely offline and requires no account or internet connection.

Introduction

The Refugee Literacy Project app ("the App") is a free educational tool designed to support literacy learning. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within the App. Your privacy matters to us, and we have built the App with that in mind.

Data We Collect

None. The App does not collect any personal or sensitive user data. Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
  • No location or GPS data
  • No photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • No contacts or address book data
  • No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprinting
  • No financial or payment information
  • No health, fitness, or biometric data
  • No browsing history or search history

Local Storage

The App saves a small amount of non-personal data directly on your device to remember your preferences:

  • Language preference – which guide language you selected
  • Lesson progress – which exercises you have completed

This data is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to any server or third party. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

Network & Internet Usage

The App is designed to work fully offline. It does not make any network requests, connect to any servers, or transmit any data from your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services. No SDKs or libraries used in the App collect or transmit user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
